Graphic Designer based in Plano, TX, and Baltimore, MD.


Due to my fascination with both art and technology at a young age, I enjoy exploring many fields of art- in both the digital and physical world. Despite my label as a graphic designer, I incorporate elements from photography, 3D design, and film-related visual effects into my personal work. I am also heavily influenced by my fine art background, using my realistic painting quality to push my digital art past the boundaries of what the eye really sees in the physical world. I value determination and hard-work, facing challenging tasks with enthusiasm as I follow my passion for graphic design. Feel free to contact me with freelance opportunities.
